Meet The Founder

Tiffany Clinton is a proud Monroe, Louisiana native who grew up on the south side of the city. Her deep connection to her community fuels a relentless passion for creating real change—especially in underserved and overlooked areas. As a visionary leader, entrepreneur, and advocate for second chances, Tiffany has dedicated her life to building programs that empower both youth and adults to rewrite their stories, unlock career opportunities, and walk boldly in their purpose.

Through her leadership in organizations like 1 Stikk Mobile Laboratory and community-centered initiatives such as Moving With a Purpose and Hustle With a Purpose, Tiffany continues to be a driving force for health equity, economic empowerment, and generational transformation throughout the South

Her mission is simple yet powerful:

"To turn struggle into strategy, pain into purpose, and hustle into hope."

Everything You Need to Know

Frequently Asked Questions

We understand you may have questions about our mobile healthcare services, training programs, and business solutions. Below are answers to the most common inquiries.

What services does 1 Stikk Mobile provide?

We provide mobile lab services including blood draws, drug & alcohol testing, genetic testing, wellness support, and specimen collection, along with training programs and business solutions.

Do you offer at-home or mobile services?

Yes. Our certified professionals come directly to your home, workplace, clinic, or facility to provide convenient and reliable healthcare services.

Do I need medical experience to join your training programs?

No. Our training programs are beginner-friendly and designed for individuals with no prior healthcare experience.

What certifications do you offer?

We offer training in phlebotomy, drug screening, mock collections, and medical assistant preparation programs with certification options.

How do I book a service or appointment?

You can book directly through our website or contact our support team at (877) 217-8455 for assistance.
