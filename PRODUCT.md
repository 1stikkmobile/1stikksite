# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Patients, families, schools, clinics, employers, and community leaders who need mobile health access, lab collection support, workforce training, and clear next steps without navigating a complicated healthcare system.

## Product Purpose

1 Stikk Mobile Inc. improves access to healthcare and training by bringing mobile lab services, health screenings, phlebotomy education, drug screening, and workforce readiness directly into underserved communities. Success means visitors understand the mission quickly, trust the organization, call for service, and book a health consultation.

## Brand Personality

Protective, mobile, and faith-rooted. The voice should feel urgent without panic, medical without coldness, and community-led without looking informal.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic Canva export pages, template healthcare stock-photo layouts, beige nonprofit brochures, sterile hospital-blue sameness, and service-site designs that hide the call or booking action.

## Design Principles

- Lead with access: every fold should make service, calling, or booking obvious.
- Make motion meaningful: animation should feel like vitals, routing, care coordination, and momentum.
- Let proof do work: real photos, certificates, training screenshots, and leadership imagery should carry credibility.
- Separate the story: each section needs a distinct role, rhythm, and visual treatment.
- Keep trust readable: high contrast, plain language, mobile-first sizing, and clear emergency boundaries.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast, keyboard-accessible navigation, reduced-motion alternatives, descriptive alt text, and clear disclaimer copy that life-threatening emergencies require calling 911.
